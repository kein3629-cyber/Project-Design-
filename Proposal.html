Help & Instructions

  • Fill in your name and student ID, then select the service you need (Enrollment, Document Request, etc.).
  • Click "Get Queue Number" to receive your number.
  • Wait for the popup notification when your number is called.
  • When your number appears, please proceed to the registrar window immediately.
  • The "Now Serving" number only increases when there are students waiting in the queue.
  • Popups and sound alerts ensure you know when it is your turn.
  • This system is designed to simplify queuing and reduce waiting confusion.
